# Unit Economics Analysis for Streamline Pro (SaaS Product)

**TechStream Solutions** is a Software as a Service (SaaS) company and their product is called **"Streamline Pro"** - A platform that provides comprehensive project management and collaboration tools for businesses of all sizes.

This primary objective of this project is to analyze the financial health of **Streamline Pro** by calculating unit economics metrics and assessing whether the company’s customer acquisition strategy as well as operational expenses is sustainable and scalable

## Unit Economics Metrics

The following key metrics were calculated:

- CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- ARPU (Average Revenue per User)
- COGS (Cost of Goods Sold)
- Gross Margin
- LTV (Customer Lifetime Value)
- LTV / CAC ratio

## Methodology

The analysis was performed using Python (Pandas) in Google Colab.

**Steps included:**

1. Loading and merging multiple operational datasets
2. Calculating total marketing spend
3. Estimating number of acquired customers
4. Computing CAC, ARPU & COG
5. Estimating gross margin using operational expenses
6. Computing customer lifetime value (LTV)
7. Evaluating business sustainability using the LTV/CAC ratio

## Results

The calculated unit economics metrics are:

- CAC: 1213.97
- ARPU: 284.36
- COG: 50,040
- Gross Margin: 39.73%
- LTV: 111,196.96
- LTV/CAC Ratio: 91.59

## **Insights and Recommendations**

Based on the calculated unit economics, **Streamline Pro** demonstrates a **strong and sustainable** business model. 

The company's **CAC** accounted for **1213** which is much higher than **ARPU** of **284** shows that the company incurred losses in the first few months. However, the **LTV/CAC** ratio of **91.6** indicates that the customer lifetime value significantly exceeds the acquisition cost, suggesting that marketing strategies are **highly efficient.** In addition, the **gross margin** of approximately **39.7%** shows that the company maintains **healthy profitability** after covering production costs. 

Overall, the results suggest that the company is operating with **strong financial efficiency** and has significant potential to scale its customer acquisition strategy.
